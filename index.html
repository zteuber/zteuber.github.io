About Me

Hello and welcome! I’m Ziwen Teuber. I’m currently a Senior Researcher at the University of Luxembourg, dedicated to advancing family research and improving family support systems. My academic journey began at Bielefeld University, where I studied psychology from 2011 to 2016 and later completed my PhD in 2021. Today, my work centers on Applied Developmental and Educational Psychology, with the goal of generating research that makes a meaningful and practical difference in family development, functioning, and well-being.

Projects

  • Scrolling Minds
  • Predictors and Outcomes of Parental Burnout and Parental Educational Involvement (POPP)
  • Parental Holiday Stress
